About Me

My journey into the world of technology began with a fascination for problem-solving and the endless possibilities that digital innovations could unlock. Growing up, I was inspired by how technology transformed industries and connected the world, motivating me to pursue a career where I could be part of this evolution.

I am Kaushik Chintalapudi, an entry-level Cloud Analyst with 1 year, 4 months and 3 days of experience in exploring cloud infrastructure and emerging technologies. A Computer Science Engineering graduate, graduated in July 2023, I am passionate about learning and applying my knowledge to solve challenges in the dynamic field of cloud computing and AI.

My experience includes working with platforms like Microsoft Azure and Microsoft Power Platform and support cloud governance initiatives. Through projects like the Chargeback Automator I’ve developed a strong foundation in cloud technologies.

I possess a diverse skill set, from Python programming to low-code app development, and I’m eager to deepen my understanding of concepts like containerization and microservices architecture. My journey reflects a commitment to staying adaptable while embracing emerging trends in technology.

Currently, I am undergoing Oracle Fusion Financials training as part of a 100% Job Guarantee Program offered by ERPTree in Hyderabad. This hands-on program is enhancing my expertise in enterprise financial management systems, complementing my cloud and AI skills for integrated business solutions.

While focused on impactful projects today, I am inspired by the potential of technology to shape the future, particularly through scalable solutions in the cloud and AI ecosystems.

Beyond work, I enjoy playing guitar, cricket, and exploring the creative world of music.

My time with the MIT NCC Troop (MNT) in college honed my leadership, discipline, and teamwork skills, which complement my technical expertise.

I also had done many courses from platforms like DeepLearning.AI, Linkedin Learning, Forage, etc.

I’m excited to connect with like-minded professionals and collaborate on innovative ideas that shape the future.
